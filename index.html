About Me

Hello! I'm Naomi, a varsity athlete and senior at #Brown University double-majoring in computer science and English. With my knowledge of computer science, I love applying machine learning and UI/UX design to create digital health solutions and improve patient care. If you catch me on a debugging break, I'll probably be writing a poem or some sort of extensive disquisition. Otherwise, you can find me searching for boba, creating digital art, or investigating human physiology and psychology.

Previously, I've interned at #Siemens Healthineers and #Intuitive Surgical in big data and full-stack software engineering. My academic research has been concentrated on delivering health solutions and exploring ethical algorithmic implications.

I'll be returning to Providence this fall to complete my M.S. in computer science through Brown's 5th-Year Baccalaureate program. As I learn, I'm always looking for opportunities to work with kind and talented people, so feel free to #reach out to me. Check out some of my projects below to see what I've been up to!
