About Me

Hello! I'm Naomi, a master's graduate with a specialization in AI/ML and Computational Biology at #Brown University. As an undergrad, I was a four-year varsity athlete and double-major in computer science and English. With my knowledge of computer science, I strive to apply data science and statistical learning to create digital health solutions that improve patient care. When I'm not debugging, you can find me writing creative nonfiction or perusing SOTA works. Otherwise, I'm searching for boba, reading paperbacks, or hitting golf balls.

Previously, I've interned at #Siemens Healthineers and #Intuitive Surgical in big data and full-stack software engineering. My academic research has been concentrated on preclinical/clinical genomics and evidence generation from RWD. Currently, I serve as a Data Scientist at #Transcarent.
