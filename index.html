About Me

Hello! I'm Naomi, a fourth-year varsity athlete at #Brown University double-majoring in computer science and English. With my knowledge of computer science, I enjoy applying machine learning and UI/UX design to create digital health solutions that improve patient care. When I'm not debugging, you can find me writing poetry or adding to my latest disquisition. Otherwise, I'm searching for boba, creating digital art, or reading novels.

Previously, I've interned at #Siemens Healthineers and #Intuitive Surgical in big data and full-stack software engineering. My academic research has been concentrated on delivering health solutions and exploring ethical algorithmic implications.

As I learn, I'm always looking for opportunities to work with kind and talented people, so feel free to #reach out to me. Check out some of my most recent projects below to see what I've been up to!
