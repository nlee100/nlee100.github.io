About Me

Hello! I'm Naomi, a 5th-year master's student specializing in AI/ML and computational biology at #Brown University. As an undergrad, I was a fourth-year varsity athlete and double-major in computer science and English. With my knowledge of computer science, I enjoy applying statistical learning and UI/UX design to create digital health solutions that improve patient care. When I'm not debugging, you can find me writing poetry or adding to my latest disquisition. Otherwise, I'm searching for boba, reading paperbacks, or hitting golf balls.

Previously, I've interned at #Siemens Healthineers and #Intuitive Surgical in big data and full-stack software engineering. My academic research has been concentrated on drug response prediction and ethical algorithmic implications. This summer, I'll be interning as a data scientist at #Transcarent.

As I learn, I'm always looking for opportunities to work with kind and talented people, so feel free to #reach out to me. Check out some of my most recent projects below to see what I've been up to!
