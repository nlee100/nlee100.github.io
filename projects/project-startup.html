UI Design: Startup Company



Task

With a group of three other Brown University students, I iteratively designed an mobile interface for the startup Real World Playbook (now called Realworld). Our design process included extensive user testing experiments and analysis, ethical considerations, and lo-fi and hi-fi sketches using Sketch and Figma. The final hi-fi Figma prototype can be seen above.

Context

#Realworld is an education-first platform that provides new undergraduates step-by-step guides for navigating post-college life. Step-by-step guides advise students about health insurance, taxes, employer paperwork, budgeting, credit, etc. to help them find independence. Currently, it is used by 40+ top universities in America.
